Nicole and family

Richard has helped us to understand our Ah Boy during the BCP training. In the past, Ah Boy is always jumping onto our sofa and ourselves. Treating Ah Boy as our own family member, he has became a ‘spoilt kid’ in the house, barked at us whenever he didn’t get what he wanted and required to be fed during meal times. Richard has taught our family through body language to have a better communication with Ah Boy in order to improve Ah Boy ‘spoilt kid’ behaviour. After the three days of 'No Talk' only body language with Ah Boy, our family have seen better improvement with Ah Boy’s behaviour. Now Ah Boy does not jump onto the sofa, ourselves and not requiring us to feed him. Even though sometimes, Ah Boy misbehaved, we have a better control over him now. Learning is always on-going. We will continue to improve and enforce what we have learnt during the BCP training. Really thank Richard for his rich knowledge sharing with the family, encouragements and constant follow-up with us. Thanks Jennifer and Family.

Janet and family

Notti, as the name says, has always been naughty since young. We pampered him till he "terriorized" us with his aggression of snappings and growlings. Frankly, we are clueless to solve his problems. Through recommendation, I called K9, Richard. He came and access Notti. As Richard said, 1) Notti is not in the red-zone agression. He can be changed. It is a 3 days' challenge ' the 3 Nos' 2) It is the Owner's fault. We must train ourselves to use more of our body language and authority energy to correct him. This calm - assertiveness is all we need to change Notti. 3) We must take over Notti's dominance to be his Pack Leader. Nothing comes easy. Notti challenged me with his growling, he even jumped and bit my hand. I maintained my calm and tug his leash. Slowly, he submitted. Previously, I would just allow him to get away. We have seen improvement in his walks. With chest up, chin up body language, walking him with his new slip training leash is a breeze now. Through this learning journey, we are committed to take the role of a Pack Leader. Thanks, Richard for helping us. We believe, we can and will transform Notti, not to be naughtly anymore.
